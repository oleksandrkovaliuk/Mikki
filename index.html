How I help you with efficient and cost-effective software solutions

A complex technology stack is a beaten path. Dozens of projects in the various domains helped to develop confidence in the approach I am taking to help my clients. Software is not only code; it is also the right process of working with business hand by hand.

Why engage me?

No need to get a full-time digital specialist on board, hire only when you need one. Resulting in less costs, high expertise in digital marketing, and a broad knowledge from different industries.

01

Build Solution

Building entires solution based on your business requirements with the step-by-step incremental iterations.

02

Optimize Cost

Evaluation of the existing infrastructure and development costs. Comprehensive advice on possible optimisation points.

03

Tech Audit

Technical analysis of the solution and listing set of possible improvements for the system with the estimated cost of development.

04

Tune the Process

Tuning development and delivery process by following the most suitable practice like Scrum, Kanban, XP, etc.

05

Remote Team

Extending your team with the remote top-notch Engineers who deliver state-of-art software solutions.

06

Mentoriship

Helping your team to be effective in their technical decisions by running set of workshops relevant to your tech stack.
